Months, even years of intensive developments; several candidates releases, a very hard work... And now Galette 0.9 begins!

Changelog for this release is quite long, I invite you to check the full changelog for details.

Even if changes are numerous for this release (which EOL 0.8 series), no database upgrade is required if you're runnig an up to date 0.8 :-)

Around this version, several services has been provided:

  • Galette's forums,
  • a feedback platform for future features,
  • a Telegram group,
  • a Telemetry app.

And of course, all existing communication channels (mailing lists, IRC channel, tracker, ...) are still available.

Please share your Telemetry informations and register as an user; this may help the project ;-)

I'd like to thank very much every one that has been involved in this release! And a very, very big thanks for those who tests candidate releases and have filled bugs that I have fixed.

I wish you all a merry Xmas and a very happy new year!
